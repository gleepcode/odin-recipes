Apple pie

Description

An apple pie is a pie in which the principal filling is apples.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Pie crust
  • Sweetener
  • Thickener
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Preheat and prep
  2. Mix the filling
  3. Prepare the bottom crust
  4. Fill the pie
  5. Add the top crust
  6. Vent and finish
  7. Bake
  8. Cool
